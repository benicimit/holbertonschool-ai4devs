# UI Concept - Study Planner App

## Purpose
Help students organize their study schedule, track upcoming assignments and exams, and manage their time effectively to improve academic performance.

## Users
- High School Students: manage classes, homework, and test preparation.
- College/University Students: organize complex course loads, lectures, deadlines, and study groups.
- Lifelong Learners: track progress on self-paced courses and certifications.

## Core Screens
- Dashboard with today's schedule and upcoming deadlines.
- Timetable/Calendar view (daily, weekly, monthly).
- Add Task/Event form (for assignments, exams, study sessions).
- Subject/Course overview (progress, reading lists, notes).

## Actions
- Add, edit, or delete a study block, assignment, or exam.
- Mark tasks or study sessions as complete.
- View daily study progress and upcoming deadlines.
- Categorize tasks by subject or urgency.
