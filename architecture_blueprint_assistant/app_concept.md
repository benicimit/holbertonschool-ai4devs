# Application Concept – CarpoolConnect

## Application Overview
CarpoolConnect is a corporate commute management platform that helps employees share rides with coworkers who have similar routes and schedules. The goal of the application is to reduce commuting costs, decrease traffic congestion, and support corporate sustainability initiatives.

The platform enables companies to manage employee commuting more efficiently by providing ride-matching technology, analytics on environmental impact, and tools that improve coordination between carpool participants.

## Core Features
- **Smart Commute Matching:** Matches employees with coworkers who have similar commute routes and schedules.
- **Corporate Single Sign-On (SSO):** Allows employees to securely log in using company credentials.
- **Environmental Impact Tracking:** Calculates CO₂ savings generated by shared rides.
- **Cost Savings Analytics:** Tracks money saved by employees and organizations through carpooling.
- **Schedule Coordination and Messaging:** Allows carpool members to communicate and coordinate commuting schedules.

## User Types
- **Employees:** Use the platform to find coworkers to share rides and reduce commuting expenses.
- **HR Managers:** Track employee participation and evaluate the benefits of commuting programs.
- **Sustainability Officers:** Monitor environmental impact and carbon reduction metrics.
- **Facility Managers:** Optimize parking resources and transportation logistics.
- **IT Administrators:** Manage system integrations and ensure secure access to the platform.

## Constraints

### Scalability
The system must support at least **10,000 users per corporate client** and handle peak usage during morning and evening commute hours.

### Compliance
The platform must comply with **data privacy regulations such as GDPR** and follow corporate security standards for employee data protection.

### Platform Support
The application must be accessible through a **web interface** and **mobile platforms (iOS and Android)**.

### Performance
Ride-matching requests should return results in **under 2 seconds** to ensure a responsive user experience.
