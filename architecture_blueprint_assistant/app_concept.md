# Application Concept – CarpoolConnect

## Application
Corporate commute management and carpooling platform that connects employees with similar travel routes and schedules to share rides, reduce commuting costs, and lower company carbon emissions.

## Core Features
- Smart commute matching based on routes, schedules, and preferences
- Corporate Single Sign-On (SSO) for secure employee access
- Environmental impact tracking and CO₂ reduction reporting
- Cost savings analytics for employees and companies
- Carpool group messaging and schedule coordination
- Parking optimization with reserved spaces for carpool vehicles

## Users
- Employees: want affordable, reliable, and social commuting options
- HR Managers: track participation and employee cost savings
- Sustainability Officers: monitor carbon reduction and environmental metrics
- Facility Managers: manage parking usage and transportation logistics
- IT Administrators: manage integrations, security, and user access

## Constraints
- Must scale to support 10,000+ users per corporate client
- GDPR and corporate security compliance required
- Response time under 2 seconds for matching requests
- Available as web platform and mobile apps (iOS and Android)
- Integration with corporate identity and enterprise systems
