# CarpoolConnect – User Stories

## 1. Smart Commute Matching
**Role:** Corporate Employee  
**Goal:** Find coworkers with similar commute routes and schedules  
**Benefit:** Share rides and reduce commuting costs

**Acceptance Criteria**
- The system suggests coworkers whose routes are within a 2-mile radius.
- Matching considers schedule flexibility of ±30 minutes.
- Users can view at least three recommended carpool matches.

**Priority:** MVP

---

## 2. Carpool Group Communication
**Role:** Corporate Employee  
**Goal:** Communicate with members of my carpool group  
**Benefit:** Coordinate rides and schedule changes easily

**Acceptance Criteria**
- Users can send and receive messages within their carpool group.
- Notifications are sent when a new message is received.
- Users can see message history for their group.

**Priority:** High

---

## 3. Schedule Coordination
**Role:** Carpool Participant  
**Goal:** Share and update my commuting schedule  
**Benefit:** Ensure reliable ride planning with my carpool group

**Acceptance Criteria**
- Users can set their preferred departure and return times.
- The system notifies group members when schedules change.
- Updated schedules automatically refresh carpool matches.

**Priority:** MVP

---

## 4. Corporate Single Sign-On (SSO)
**Role:** IT Administrator  
**Goal:** Allow employees to log in using company credentials  
**Benefit:** Ensure secure and seamless platform access

**Acceptance Criteria**
- Employees can log in using their corporate identity provider.
- User accounts are automatically created after first login.
- Access permissions are assigned based on employee roles.

**Priority:** MVP

---

## 5. Environmental Impact Dashboard
**Role:** Sustainability Officer  
**Goal:** Track carbon emission reductions from carpooling  
**Benefit:** Measure progress toward sustainability goals

**Acceptance Criteria**
- The system calculates CO₂ savings for each carpool trip.
- Users can view monthly environmental impact reports.
- Reports can be exported for sustainability reporting.

**Priority:** High

---

## 6. Cost Savings Tracking
**Role:** HR Manager  
**Goal:** Monitor how much employees save by carpooling  
**Benefit:** Demonstrate value of corporate commuting programs

**Acceptance Criteria**
- The platform calculates estimated cost savings per employee.
- HR can view aggregated cost savings across the company.
- Reports can be downloaded for management review.

**Priority:** High

---

## 7. Parking Space Reservation
**Role:** Facility Manager  
**Goal:** Reserve parking spots for carpool vehicles  
**Benefit:** Encourage carpool participation and reduce congestion

**Acceptance Criteria**
- Carpool drivers can reserve designated parking spaces.
- The system displays real-time parking availability.
- Parking usage reports are available to facility managers.

**Priority:** Medium

---

## 8. Carpool Preference Settings
**Role:** Corporate Employee  
**Goal:** Set preferences for my carpool experience  
**Benefit:** Be matched with compatible coworkers

**Acceptance Criteria**
- Users can specify preferences such as smoking, music, or conversation.
- The matching algorithm considers these preferences when suggesting carpools.
- Users can update preferences at any time.

**Priority:** Medium
